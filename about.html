    Our Mission

    Raghvendra Kumar, also known as Delhi's Helmetman, is dedicated to promoting road safety awareness and advocating for responsible behavior among two-wheeler riders. Fueled by a deeply personal experience of losing a dear friend in a tragic accident, Kumar's mission is to prevent similar incidents and spare families the anguish of losing their loved ones due to preventable accidents. He aims to achieve this by emphasizing the importance of wearing helmets and adhering to traffic rules, thereby contributing to a safer environment for all road users.

    Our Vision

    Raghvendra Kumar envisions a future where every two-wheeler rider understands the critical role helmets play in safeguarding lives. He strives for a society where wearing a helmet is not just a legal obligation but a conscious choice made by every individual. Kumar's vision extends to comprehensive road safety education, where people are not only aware of the rules but also actively engage in responsible behavior, ensuring the roads are secure for everyone.
